Dear LU Supporter,

My name is Catherine Havasi, and I'm on the board of Learning Unlimited. In 2010, four new Splash or related programs began at Harvard, Duke, Northwestern, and Boston College, and LU programs reached over 5000 students. We're on track to reach 50,000 students annually by 2015.

I volunteer for LU because I've taught at Splash. I've seen my students' eyes light up after they've just learned something amazing. I can see what a difference it makes to attend a Splash. Although each local program is primarily self-funded, LU still needs donations and support in order to help those local programs and to continue to mentor new universities in starting a Splash.

You may have noticed that Splash registration is sometimes slow, or buggy, or confusing: that's why LU is looking to hire a web developer to improve the software that chapters use. Donations also go to improve teacher training, to support program mentoring, and other work critical to the success of Splash.

Please think of us when making your yearly charitable donations. We cannot continue to run without your help and support. You can donate at:
