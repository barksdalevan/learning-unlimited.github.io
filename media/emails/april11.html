Stanford Splash Teacher Jeff Simon To Join Teach for America

Jeff Simon teaching "Fun With Chemistry!"

Several years ago, Jeff Simon arrived at Stanford University with a passion for chemistry and an interest in making the world a better place. Jeff heard about Stanford Splash through an e-mail, and decided to join, despite having no previous teaching experience.

"At the time, I was worried about teaching, but I wanted to do it," Jeff said. So he signed up to be a part of Stanford Splash.

After teaching a hands-on chemistry class that involves smoke, explosions, and funny sounds to students at Stanford Splash and at underserved schools in the Bay Area, Jeff decided he was ready for the next step: he has committed to teaching high school chemistry in Newark, NJ.
