Learning Unlimited's Privacy Policy

This is the web site of Learning Unlimited.

The information we collect through our site registration process is used to support programs and activities conducted by Learning Unlimited ("LU") and its member organizations. Additionally, it is used to improve these programs and to improve the content of our Web page and to contact users about LU activities and programs run by members of LU.

We may share or publish anonymous information (such as data from surveys) and anonymized aggregate information (such as the number of members on various mailing lists), for any purposes that further LU's mission. We will never share information that could be used to identify a particular person (including their e-mail address, mailing address, or phone number) without their consent. We will never sell or rent any information.

In case of emergency (including but not limited to medical emergencies), we will release all necessary information to any authorities who require it (such as police or medical professionals).

We do not store credit-card numbers, or any other information that would immediately permit financial transactions, on our servers. We will never disclose this information because we do not have it. LU accepts donations through Paypal, a third-party provider; their privacy policy can be found here.

If you do not want to be contacted by us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at and we will immediately remove you from our contact lists.
