Free E-Book on Internship and Job Searches

Longtime Learning Unlimited volunteer Amy Estersohn has written a guide for job and internship seekers.

“I’ve reviewed about 200 resumes and cover letters of extremely talented college students, but very few of them know how to assemble an effective resume or conduct a strategic search,” Estersohn writes. “As a supporter of Learning Unlimited, I offer this guide to you as a way of saying thanks.”
