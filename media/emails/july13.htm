Teacher Camp Encourages Splash Leaders to Collaborate, Rethink Education

This June, Learning Unlimited supported expenses for Splash leaders to attend a Teacher Camp in Chicago. The program, organized by LU volunteers, featured collaborations with the Chicago Public Schools and arts and theater organizations across the city of Chicago.

Teachers and leaders from Columbia, Southwestern, Boston College, MIT, Yale, Stanford, Northwestern, and University of Chicago Splash programs attended.
