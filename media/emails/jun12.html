Medfield High School Student SHINES with help from LU

Medfield (MA) High School junior and MIT Splash and participant Ashley Shen wanted to bring Splash to her school ever since she took an organic chemistry class through Splash.

“In a traditional classroom, there’s a set body of knowledge. You eat and you spit it right back out to the teacher. At a program like Splash, you can choose what you want to learn. You digest the material,”she said.

With help from LU volunteer Jordan Moldow and LU CEO Dan Zaharopol, Ashley ran SHINE, a daylong event for elementary school students in Medfield. Ashley had access to LU’s program planning materials and received guidance on everything from student recruitment to website suggestions.
