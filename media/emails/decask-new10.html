Dear LU Supporter,

My name is Roshini Zachariah, and I'm a volunteer for Learning Unlimited. LU is a nonprofit organization dedicated to spreading and supporting Splash programs all over the country.

I volunteer for LU because I know what a difference it makes for a teenager to find something they're passionate to learn about, and I can't imagine a better place to do it than Splash, where you can literally learn about everything. This year LU reached 5000 students, and we're on track to reach 50,000 students by 2015.

Please think of us when making your yearly charitable donations. We cannot continue to run without your help and support. You can donate at:
